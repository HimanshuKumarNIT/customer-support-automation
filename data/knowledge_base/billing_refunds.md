# Refund Policy and Timelines

Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase if the product
is unused and in original condition. Refunds are processed to the original payment
method within 5-7 business days after approval.

Digital/subscription products are refundable within 14 days of the initial charge,
provided less than 10% of the usage quota has been consumed.

To request a refund, the customer must provide the order ID and reason for return.
Refunds for orders older than 30 days require manager approval and are handled
on a case-by-case basis.

Common triggers: "refund", "money back", "charged twice", "cancel my order",
"return this item".
