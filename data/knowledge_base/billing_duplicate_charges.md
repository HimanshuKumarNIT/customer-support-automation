# Duplicate or Incorrect Charges

If a customer reports being charged twice for the same order, first verify the
transaction IDs in the billing dashboard. Duplicate charges caused by payment
gateway retries are automatically reversed within 3-5 business days.

If the duplicate charge does not reverse automatically after 5 business days,
escalate to the Billing team with the transaction IDs and order number.

For incorrect amounts (e.g. wrong currency conversion, coupon not applied),
issue a partial refund for the difference after verifying the original order
total in the order management system.

Common triggers: "charged twice", "double charge", "wrong amount", "overcharged",
"billing error".
