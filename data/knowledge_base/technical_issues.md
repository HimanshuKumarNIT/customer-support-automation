# App Crashes and Technical Errors

For app crashes on launch, first ask the customer to confirm their app version
and device OS version. Most crash issues are resolved by updating to the latest
app version or restarting the device.

If the crash persists after updating, ask the customer to clear the app cache
(Android) or offload/reinstall the app (iOS), which resolves the majority of
corrupted-cache-related crashes without requiring a data reset.

For error codes shown to the customer (e.g. ERR_5023, ERR_1092), check the
internal error code registry - these usually indicate a payment gateway timeout
or a temporary server-side issue that resolves within 15 minutes.

Common triggers: "app keeps crashing", "error code", "not loading", "app is
frozen", "bug report".
