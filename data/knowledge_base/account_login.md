# Account Access and Login Issues

If a customer cannot log in, first confirm they are using the correct email
address associated with their account. Password reset links are sent via email
and expire after 60 minutes.

For accounts locked after multiple failed login attempts, the lock automatically
lifts after 30 minutes. If the customer needs immediate access, verify their
identity with order history or account creation date, then manually unlock the
account.

Two-factor authentication issues (not receiving SMS codes) are usually resolved
by checking the phone number on file is correct and asking the customer to
request a new code after 60 seconds.

Common triggers: "can't log in", "forgot password", "account locked", "2FA not
working", "reset my password".
