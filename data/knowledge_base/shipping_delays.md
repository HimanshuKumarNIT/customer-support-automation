# Shipping Times and Lost Packages

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days domestically and 10-15 business days
internationally. Expedited shipping (2-3 business days) is available at checkout
for an additional fee.

If a package shows as "delivered" but the customer has not received it, ask them
to check with neighbors and building management, then wait 48 hours before filing
a lost package claim, since carriers sometimes mark items delivered early.

If a package is delayed beyond the estimated delivery window by more than 5 days,
the customer is eligible for a shipping cost refund or a replacement shipment,
whichever they prefer.

Common triggers: "where is my order", "package not delivered", "shipping delay",
"lost package", "tracking not updating".
